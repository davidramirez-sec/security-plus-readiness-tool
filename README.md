# Security+ Readiness Tool

A study tool for SY0-701 that asks a few questions about where you’re at, then gives you a study plan that actually matches your situation.

It’s built for people who want a clearer path to Security+ instead of random advice, generic timelines, or one-size-fits-all study lists.

## What it does

- Gives you a quick readiness check
- Breaks down weaker domains
- Builds a study plan based on your background and timeline
- Points you toward useful resources and next steps
- Helps you figure out whether you need foundation work, a structured plan, a retake reset, or a short sprint

## Who it’s for

- People starting from scratch
- People with IT experience but new to security
- Retakers trying to fix the exact gaps that got them last time
- Anyone who wants a more focused Security+ prep plan

## How to use it

Open the app, answer the questions, and review the plan it generates.

That’s it.

## Why I made it

Most Security+ study advice online is too broad. A beginner, a retaker, and someone with real IT experience should not all be following the same plan.

I wanted something that feels more like a diagnostic than a generic study checklist.

## Notes

This project is still being improved, so I’ll probably keep adjusting the questions, recommendations, and overall flow over time.
